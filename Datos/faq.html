RoyaleReport is an app that allows you to view Clash Royale cards, check their statistics, see the best decks, and read the game news. Additionally, you can view your own statistics and those of your friends.

Don't worry, you can view the statistics of any Clash Royale player; you just need to search for their username.

You won't be able to link your Clash Royale user account with the app, so you won't be able to see the upcoming chests you'll receive.
